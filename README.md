## Simple Workable Job Scraper

Our **Simple Workable Job Scraper** is a fast and efficient tool designed to extract job listings from any company using Workable for their career page. If you need clean, essential job data without the complexity of a heavy-duty scraper, this actor is the perfect solution.

Designed with simplicity in mind, this actor focuses on extracting only the most critical information, delivering a minimal and highly usable dataset. It's ideal for users who need to quickly gather job data for analysis, populate a database, or perform market research.

### Who is this for?
* **Recruiters** looking for a simple way to track job openings at specific companies.
* **Data Analysts** who need quick, structured data on the job market.
* **Developers** who want a straightforward data source for a job board or application.
* **Job Seekers** wanting to automate their search across multiple Workable-powered companies.

### What Input is Required?
All you need to provide is the URL of a company's career page that is hosted on Workable. For example: `https://apply.workable.com/asana/`.

### What Data Will I Get?
This actor provides a clean, focused dataset with the following minimal columns for each job found:

* `jobTitle`: The title of the job position.
* `companyName`: The name of the company.
* `location`: The city, state, and/or country of the job.
* `jobUrl`: A direct link to the job posting.
